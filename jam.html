ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Team Size 4-6

JAM is Just A Minute. It has evolved from the BBC radio show of the same name (which has been broadcast for the last 40 odd years!). The JAMmers are challenged to speak for one minute on a given subject without “Repetition, Hesitation, or Deviation“. Just-A-Minute (or JAM) is an all round-fun event that is all about the control of the mind over the mouth. Can you make it through sixty seconds of non-stop talking without hesitation, repetition, or deviation? Or will the sheer pressure make you crumble and have your competitors pounce on you in an instant?

There will be 2 rounds - PRELIMINARY & FINAL Rounds . The Last round will be only among only 6 participants .Participants will pick up a chit, which will contain three diverse topics. Among the three topics, he/she will choose one topic. To collect food for thought/ideas every participant will get two minutes. After two minutes, the speaker has to speak continuous one minute on the chosen topic, standing on the podium, before the judges and audience. Time duration given to the participant will be JUST A MINUTE. Judging criteria will be based on choice of points, confidence, pronunciation & enunciation, grammar, choice of words, body language and attire (formal). From there the winner will be declared.
