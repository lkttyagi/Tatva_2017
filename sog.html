Stroke OF Genius

ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Team size 2

Teams from different colleges will compete in a thrilling competition of business acumen among budding managers. Teams will have to be prepared with the recent as well as past significant business related issues, punch lines, logos etc.All the different rules of the quiz will be communicated during the respective rounds.

RULES

  • Round 1
    Level 1
    Current Economic Information
    You can ask questions indicating Economics, Markets et cetera.
    Level 2
    LOGO identification
    Get some logos which are not easily identifiable. Passing is there; no multiple choice; Negative marking is there.
    Round 2
    Level 1
    Current Affairs/General Knowledge
    No passing of questions, No negative marking.
    Level 2
    Tag line identification
    Get some amazing taglines. This should be a slightly tougher one.
    Passing is there; no multiple choice ; Negative marking is there. Scores can be +10 and -5 for negative.
    Round 3
    Level 1
    Guess the Quotes from Corporates like Mr.NRN, Azim premzi , Kiran mazumdar shaw ...etc....etc...Negative can be there or no negative.
    Level 2
    RAPID FIRE ROUND 90seconds 5 questions per team
