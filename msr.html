Market Situation & Reaction

.

ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Team size 3

Giving specific situation to the budding managers and they have to decide what step they will take to overcome the particular situation. You have to apply your marketing knowledge to overcome the problem. Example- Company is facing financial losses because of introduction of new product, now u have to decide what step and how you can improve , whether to close it down or anything else.

RULES

  • Competitors may not use cell phones or smart devices during the event.
  • Except for team events competitors must work independently, without assistance from teachers, fellow participants. Participants may be disqualified for receiving assistance.
  • The distribution of event samples, material or thank you notes to judges, is not permitted.
  • After the completion of one round elimination will be there.
  • There can be three members from one group
  • Time provided for first round will be 5 minutes and in second and final round 4 minutes will be given
  • There will be three rounds and will be of ascending order in terms of complexity and difficulty in questions. Questions will be framed later.
