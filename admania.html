Admania

.

ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Team size 4

This event is about making ad in your own creation. Basically , we give you the name of product on spot and supporting prop with it. You will be given 2:30hr time to prepare it with in the campus. There will be three rounds.
ROUND 1
The participants are supposed to make ad with the help of props and make an ad to sell the product.
ROUND 2
Make a tag line/ jingle/rhyme song for your product.
ROUND 3
Rapid fire/ Question round. Questions will be asked about the product they have made.
RULES
The ad must be entertaining
The set time for enacting an ad will be 2 minutes
Any team exceeding the time limit will be discarded
No indecency in the ad will be entertained
Usage of prop is compulsory
The ad should not be copied

Student should bring the stationery requirement for the events themselves other than the basic.
