ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Team size 6

The event is based on the making of taglines for various product. Participants has to select displayed product through chits chosen by them. There are two rounds for this competiotion and within 30 minutes they have to make innovative tagline for that product in ist round. Second round is a twist for the teams.

RULES
The timing should be properly followed
Participants should maintain proper discipline during event
There should be no use of abusive words in the tagline by the participants
There should be healthy competition among teams that is no copying of taglines
Participants has to select displayed product according to their interest in twenty seconds and within 3 minutes they have to make innovative tagline for that product
Not more than 4 members are to be allowed in one team
Awards
The wining team would be certified and prizes will be given
Rest of the participants would be provided with the participation/appreciation certificates

Student should bring the stationery requirement for the events themselves other than the basic.
