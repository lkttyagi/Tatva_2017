ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Team size 3

Welcome to hell is a name given to the panel discussion by the students. The participants are challenged to speak for three to five minutes on a specific topic without “Repetition, Hesitation or Deviation”. Welcome to hell is an all round -fun event that is all about the control of the mind over the mouth. Can you make it through five minutes of non-stop talking without hesitation, repetition, or deviation? Or will the sheer pressure make you crumble and have your competitors pounce on you in an instant?

Student should bring the stationery requirement for the events themselves other than the basic.

RULES

  • There will be three rounds and each round will be for 20 minutes
    Each round will have three different topics to speak on.
    The participants are required to give their views on the respective topics.
    The judgement will be on the basis of the facts mentioned, clarity about the topic you are speaking, body language.
