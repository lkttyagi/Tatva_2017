Pro Curious

.

ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Team size 2-3

The event is based on mingling clues flashed over projector and creating a company name with effective punch line and then designs a well –defined logo by using managerial skills in the form of presentation.
In round 1
participants have to choose their number from the given chits which will give them a clue.
Clues will be shown to them through a presentation from which they have to select their clue according to their number.
The time duration for round 1 will be 30 minutes.
In round 2
participants have to decide their company’s logo which must associate with the clue and decides a Tagline for their clue.
For this 10 minutes will be given to the participants.
5 minutes will be given to each team for presenting their work.
Evaluation will be based on the creativity and innovative ideas and accordingly awards will be given to the team who presents its bests.

Student should bring the stationery requirement for the events themselves other than the basic.
